My path in three beats

1 · From people to systems

I started in development — UN & non-profits — working with displaced communities and social-impact programmes. That’s where I learned the core of design research: empathy, context, and listening before solving.

2 · The Moovr spark

Co-founding Moovr in Kenya, an investor asked: “Who’s the tech person on your team?” That question changed everything. I realised understanding people wasn’t enough — I needed to speak the language of tech and business.

3 · Front-row to change

Across industries, one truth holds: people evolve faster than products. My job is to keep that evolution visible — translating human shifts into design direction.
